WHO AM I 2025

Muhammad Aliff
bin Md Khairani

My name is Muhammad Aliff bin Md Khairani, but most people know me by my nickname, Yumezz. I am 20 years old and was born at Hospital Sultanah Aminah, Johor Bahru. I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Library Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Merbok, Kedah. Through my studies, I have gained knowledge and skills in information management, library services, cataloging, and digital resources.

I come from a small and loving family. My father's name is Md Khairani bin Majid, and my mother's name is Norizan binti Md Yusuf. As the only child in the family, I have learned to be independent, responsible, and self-motivated from a young age.

I currently live in Masai, Johor — a place I call home where I spend most of my time relaxing and enjoying my hobbies such as building Gundam. Apart from that, I also enjoy listening to music during my free time. Music helps me relax, clear my mind, and create a positive atmosphere, especially after a busy day of studying.

As a student and lifelong learner, I am always eager to gain new knowledge, improve my skills, and explore new opportunities. This website serves as a platform to share my background, interests, experiences, and achievements while documenting my journey toward a successful future.
